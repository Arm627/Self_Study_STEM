This page is Curriculum for a Solid to An Advanced Understanding of Subjects

This Curriculum assumes you have a solid high school level math understanding of algebra and trigonomtry. Take as long or as little as you need. On the videos I recommend watching at 2x to 3x speed. Work up to it. I recommend first watching this video to get tips of how to study faster. A good rule of thumb is two - four days per chapter. The chapters or sections you dont fully understand leave around 4 - 7 days (or as needed) to review along with do excerise problems.
Contact me if the pdf links are not working

For a wholeistic view of the subjects check out these maps; complimentary of Domain Science : Physics Map; Mathematics Map ; Computer Science Map ; Biology Map ; Chemistry Map ; and for a wholeistic map of all sciences Map of Sciences

This wont be easy but put your mind to it, be consistent and it will be done. I recommend going in order if you are not advanced with the subjects
